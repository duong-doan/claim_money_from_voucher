When you connect your TikTok account, we request access to your basic profile information and permission to upload and publish videos on your behalf via TikTok's official Content Posting API. We do not store your TikTok credentials. We only use the permissions you explicitly grant.

We collect your TikTok open_id and display name to identify your connected account. Video content you upload is transmitted directly to TikTok and not permanently stored on our servers.

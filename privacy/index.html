Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

DoanWatch ("we", "us", or "our") operates the DoanWatch platform at doan-watch.netlify.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to DoanWatch, we collect the following information via TikTok's official OAuth2 API:

• Your TikTok open_id (a unique identifier assigned by TikTok)
• Your TikTok display name and avatar
• An access token that allows us to publish videos on your behalf

We do not collect your TikTok password or any sensitive account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

• To authenticate your TikTok account and display your profile in the app
• To upload and publish video content to your TikTok account via the Content Posting API
• To maintain your session and remember your connected account

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

3. TikTok Integration

DoanWatch uses the following TikTok API permissions:

• user.info.basic — to read your basic TikTok profile information
• video.upload — to upload video files to TikTok
• video.publish — to publish videos directly to your TikTok profile

These permissions are requested through TikTok's official Login Kit and Content Posting API. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your TikTok account settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data transmissions between DoanWatch and TikTok are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Access tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.

Video content you upload is transmitted directly to TikTok's servers and is not permanently stored on our servers beyond the upload process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time by clicking "Disconnect TikTok" in the app, which will remove your access token from our system.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
• Access the personal data we hold about you
• Request deletion of your personal data
• Revoke TikTok permissions at any time
• Disconnect your TikTok account from our platform

7. Children's Privacy

DoanWatch is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
privacy@doan-watch.netlify.app

By using DoanWatch, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
